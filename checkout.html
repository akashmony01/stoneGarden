Delivery

The Stone & Garden Company aim to deliver all orders within 2-3 working days from receipt of your order (Most orders placed before 12pm will be delivered in 2 working days). Any NEXT DAY deliveries need to be placed before 12pm Monday to Thursday. Next Day deliveries placed on a Friday before 12pm will be delivered the following Monday (excluding Bank Holidays - then the delivery will be on the Tuesday)
