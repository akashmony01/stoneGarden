Plum Slate Description

We can supply a wide range of slates chippings in various sizes & colours; these include Plum Slate Chippings, Blue Slate Chippings, Green Slate Chipping and Grey Slate Chippings. These stunning slate chippings are idea for projects such as garden borders or water features as they are very durable and make your garden look neat and modern.

Plum Slate Colour and Shape

Slate is a naturally quarried product which may result in slight variations in formation, size, colour or shape.

Availability on Plum Slate

We can usually accommodate a next day delivery service if the product is ordered before 1pm for an additional cost. Normal delivery is 2-3 working days from when the order is placed. The delivery is also subject to weather conditions and stock levels. All supplied in Bulk Bags (850KG) or Sealed bags (20KG). We also supply a sample which is a small poly bag sent via first class post.

Important Delivery Information
1 Delivery Method

All deliveries are made by an 18 tonne tail lift vehicle, which must have sufficient access to the front of the delivery point and room to manoeuvre around safely. Vehicles will not reverse onto our customers property.

2 Delivery Offload

Hand Pump pallet trucks are used to take the pallets off the truck and CAN NOT operate on loose or soft ground

3 Sufficient Width

Our vehicles require a minimum width of 3 meters for delivery.

4 Reasonably Level Ground

We require a reasonable level ground, to fulfil the delivery Ideally we require the road as flat as possible, if in doubt call our sales team.

5 Free of obstacles

Obstacle free access point for a successful delivery No low hanging trees or parked cars.
